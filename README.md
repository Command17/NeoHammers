## About 📖

This mod adds hammers that let you mine large areas in no time!

Hammers are new multi-tool items. They allow you to mine *almost* anything!
But they don't allow you large areas yet. That's what the new enchantment,
*Hammering*, is for.

*Hammering* has a total of 6 levels. Each level increases the area that can
be mined with a hammer. For the first 3 levels, it increases the mining area to 3x3,
with the depth being based on the level. Starting at level 4,
it enlarges the mining area to 5x5.

You can get *Hammering* 1 to 3 from an enchanting table or a librarian. Every level after 3 can only
be obtained from ominous vaults.

## FAQ ❓

**Where can I see the recipes for this mod?**

You can either install a recipe viewer like JEI/EMI or use Minecraft's recipe book.

**What's going to happen to Hammering?**

*It's being archived*. Since Architectury has barely received ports or updates,
it's becoming increasingly difficult to work with. Because of this,
I've spent a lot of time thinking about the future of my (three) mods and how
to maintain them.
At first, I considered creating another library, but I decided against that because
of some other factors.
In the end, I decided to switch to NeoForge. It offers many useful utilities
for this mod and is updated more frequently than Architectury.
